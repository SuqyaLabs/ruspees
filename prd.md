This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the development of a landing page for **Ruspee’s**, a fast-food brand focused on quality, modern taste, and accessibility.

---

## 1. Product Overview

The goal is to create a responsive landing page that establishes a professional digital presence for Ruspee's. The page serves as a brand showcase, location finder, and a bridge to their social media and delivery services.

* 
**Brand Name:** Ruspee’s / روسبي.


* 
**Tagline:** "La qualité, au plus près de votre quotidien" (Quality, close to your daily life).


* 
**Target Market:** Fast-food consumers in Algiers (specifically Ain Allah and Draria).



---

## 2. Target Audience

* 
**Local Foodies:** Residents of Algiers looking for high-quality, "crousty" fried chicken and burgers.


* 
**On-the-go Professionals:** Users looking for quick ways to order, pick up, or get delivery.


* 
**Social Media Users:** People who discover food through TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.



---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Navigation & Branding

* 
**Logo Placement:** The Ruspee's logo must be prominent in the header.


* 
**Menu Links:** Clear navigation for "A Propos" (About), "Où nous trouver" (Locations), and "Nous contacter" (Contact).


* 
**Language Support:** Content is primarily French with Arabic brand representation.



### 3.2 Hero Section

* 
**Visual Content:** High-resolution image of a chicken bucket and drink against an Algiers-style cityscape background.


* 
**Slider Functionality:** Indicators at the bottom suggest a carousel of promotional images.



### 3.3 Brand Philosophy & Values

* 
**Quality Content:** Text sections detailing the selection of ingredients and precise cooking techniques.


* 
**Operations:** Information regarding reliable partners and trained teams.


* 
**Keywords/Motto:** Display of core brand pillars: "CROUSTY, ROSTY, TASTY".



### 3.4 Location Finder (Store Locator)

* **Current Locations:** Detailed cards for:
* 
**Ain Allah (Alger)**.


* 
**Draria (Alger)**.




* 
**Interaction:** "OBTENIR UN ITINÉRAIRE" (Get Directions) button for each location, linking to Google Maps.



### 3.5 Social Media Integration

* 
**Social Sidebar/Footer:** Links to TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.



---

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements

| Feature | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| **Mobile Responsiveness** | The site must be optimized for mobile users who are likely ordering on the go.

 |
| **Visual Identity** | Dominant red and white color scheme consistent with the brand's packaging.

 |
| **Performance** | Fast loading times to ensure users can quickly find locations or contact info.

 |
| **SEO** | Local SEO optimization for keywords like "Ruspee's Alger," "Fried chicken Draria," etc. |

---

## 5. User Stories

* 
**As a customer,** I want to see the quality of the food visually so that I feel confident in my purchase.


* 
**As a local resident,** I want to get directions to the nearest branch (Ain Allah or Draria) with one click.


* 
**As a social media user,** I want to easily find the Ruspee’s Instagram or TikTok to see their latest deals.



---

1. **Phase 1:** UI/UX design based on the red/white brand palette.
2. **Phase 2:** Development of the landing page (HTML/CSS/JS or CMS).
